Shield Support - Staffing Detailed Overview

2. Staffing Services
Specialized staffing tiers to build your ideal technical team.

*   NOC Staffing (Network Operations Center)
    - Description: Expert teams dedicated to the 24/7 monitoring and management of your network infrastructure.
    - Roles & Responsibilities:
        - Continuous monitoring of network health and performance.
        - Incident response and fault management.
        - Reporting and documentation of network events.
        - Coordination with third-party vendors and ISPs.
    - Ideal For: MSPs and enterprises needing round-the-clock eyes on their critical infrastructure.

*   Cyber Security Staffing
    - Description: Highly trained security professionals focused on protecting your digital environment.
    - Roles & Responsibilities:
        - Security Operations Center (SOC) analysts.
        - Threat hunters and incident responders.
        - Compliance analysts (HIPAA, GDPR, SOC2).
        - Security engineers for tool implementation.
    - Ideal For: Organizations with strict compliance needs or those facing high threat levels.

*   Cloud Computing Staffing
    - Description: Specialists in designing, migrating, and managing cloud environments.
    - Roles & Responsibilities:
        - Cloud Architects (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud).
        - DevOps engineers for automation and CI/CD pipelines.
        - Cloud migration specialists.
        - Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) developers.
    - Ideal For: Businesses moving to the cloud or optimizing their existing cloud footprint.

*   Help Desk Staffing
    - Description: Front-line support agents who serve as the face of your IT department.
    - Roles & Responsibilities:
        - Level 1: Ticket triage, password resets, basic troubleshooting.
        - Level 2: Desktop support, software installation, complex issue resolution.
        - Level 3: Advanced problem solving, server-side troubleshooting, root cause analysis.
    - Ideal For: Scaling support teams to handle increased ticket volume and improving customer satisfaction scores.
